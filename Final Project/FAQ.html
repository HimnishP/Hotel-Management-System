Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. What are the check-in and check-out times?

Check-in is at 3:00 PM and check-out is at 11:00 AM. If you need a different arrangement, please contact the front desk.

2. Do you offer free Wi-Fi?

Yes, complimentary Wi-Fi is available in all guest rooms and common areas.

3. Is breakfast included with my stay?

Our hotel offers a complimentary breakfast for all guests from 7:00 AM to 10:00 AM in the dining area.

4. Can I cancel or modify my reservation?

Reservations can be canceled or modified up to 24 hours before your check-in date without any charge. Please refer to our cancellation policy for more details.

5. Do you allow pets in the hotel?

We are a pet-friendly hotel! There is an additional fee for pets, and certain restrictions apply. Please contact us for more information.

6. Is parking available at the hotel?

Yes, we offer both valet and self-parking options. Parking fees may apply.

7. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit cards, debit cards, and cash. Please note that a valid credit card is required to hold your reservation.
